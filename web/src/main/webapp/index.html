What's it all about?

The Village App is a tool for discovering what people are doing around you, as well as a concise and simple way to keep in touch with your community.

There's always something happening somewhere in the Village!

Who's in the Village?

We're creative souls at heart. We make things from scratch. We iterate on ideas until they're truly great. Usually, we're out and about perfecting our skills or tinkering on some project. Sometimes, we collaborate on projects.

What makes it so special?

We believe that when we know a little bit more about each other, fantastical things happen, making our community and our Earth a more exciting place to be.
